Terms

Plain terms for the optional Sift Plus subscription.

Subscription

Sift Plus costs 89 NOK per month and includes 200 AI requests per calendar month. A video summary and each follow-up chat answer count as one request. Unused requests do not roll over. The secure Stripe checkout shows the final recurring price, tax, renewal terms, and available payment methods before purchase.

Renewal and cancellation

Sift Plus renews monthly until canceled. You can manage or cancel it from Sift Settings. Access continues according to the cancellation date shown by Stripe. Choosing Delete hosted account cancels the subscription immediately and deletes Sift's hosted account records.

Payments and refunds

Sift is the seller and Stripe processes payment, tax calculation, and invoices. Statutory consumer rights are not limited by these terms. For a billing problem, use the Stripe receipt or contact Sift support.

AI answers

AI output can be incomplete or wrong. Verify important medical, legal, financial, safety, and technical information against reliable sources. Do not use Sift Plus to process content you are not allowed to share with an AI provider.

Availability and acceptable use

Sift may apply reasonable technical limits to protect the service. Do not share installation credentials, automate bulk requests, bypass quotas, attack the service, or use it unlawfully. Sift may suspend access necessary to stop abuse or protect other users.

Changes and support

Material changes will be posted here. For support, open an issue on the Sift support page.
